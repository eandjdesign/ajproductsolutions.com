A&J Product Solutions is a full service prototyping, model making, and mold making company founded by Art Villa and Jaime Palominos.

Art Villa has been working in the prototype business since 1983. His extensive experience includes General Manager of Nadi, Production Manager at Cameltech, Production Manager at Satellite Models, and then Director of Operations at Contour. In 2001 Art joined Jaime to form A&J Product Solutions.

Jaime Palominos has worked in the prototype business since 1986. His experience includes Nadi, Assistant production manager at Cameltech, Assistant Production Manager at Satellite Models, and then Production Manager at Contour. In 2001 Jaime joined Art to form A&J Product Solutions.

Art & Jaime have had a long working relationship in the prototype business. Together they have 37 years of experience in prototyping, model making, and mold making. They have established a reputation for high quality and excellent relationships with their clients.

“What makes us unique to this type of business is how close we work with our clients in order to be sure we understand the needs of their project and be sure they are satisfied with the result.”

A&J Product Solutions looks forward to working to meet your prototyping needs.From polymer castings of prototype parts to short run productions, our capable staff is ready to work with you personally on your next project.
