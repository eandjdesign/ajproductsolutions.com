Your One-Stop Solution

A+J Product Solutions is a thirteen person consumer electronics product prototyping company focused on building long-term client relationships.

The company was started by Art Villa in 1998 with a focus on producing urethane castings for prototype uses. It has since evolved into a one-stop service center to help product developers and designers tell their stories, pitch their ideas, confirm its manufacturing activities and make real parts.

A+J has helped over 50 companies, large and small, from start-ups to design studios to major corporations, develop their product line and build their business.
